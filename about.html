About CPS Lab

The CPS (Cyber-Physical Systems) Lab is a research and innovation hub dedicated to exploring the integration of computation with physical processes. At our university, the CPS Lab is a collaborative initiative to advance intelligent systems, smart automation, and embedded systems aligned with Industry 4.0.

Vision of the CPS Lab

Our vision is to drive interdisciplinary research and foster innovation in cyber-physical systems. We aim to empower students and researchers by providing them with hands-on experience, mentorship, and exposure to real-world challenges.

Primary Activities of CPS Lab

Shoolini University CPS lab

The Shoolini University CPS lab co-powered by AWaDH, Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, part of the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM ICPS), GoI, drive CPS advancements within partner institutes. We promote research, development, and deployment of CPS technologies, enabling students, entrepreneurs, and researchers to gain hands-on experience and expertise in this transformative field.

Internship Carnivals

Welcome to the Internship Carnival at CPS AWaDH, a premier initiative designed to bridge the gap between enthusiastic students and industry professionals in the realm of cyber-physical systems (CPS), agriculture, and water technologies. This vibrant platform offers participants the unique opportunity to delve into hands-on projects and gain invaluable insights into the latest advancements.

Conferences & Workshop

Every conference organized through Shoolini University CPS lab and AWaDH will focus on topics connected to a technology innovation domain in AgriTech and the related concerns. Out of two conferences planned every year, one conference will be of internal nature to Gain global competitiveness and international visibility.

CHANAKYA Initiative

The CHANAKYA initiative, formally known as the Comprehensive and Holistic Advancement of National Knowledge Yield and Analytics (CHANAKYA) aims to foster the widespread adoption of Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) in India. Through unique fellowships, CHANAKYA aims to empower researchers and professionals to integrate CPS technologies deeply into the fabric of Indian industries and society.
